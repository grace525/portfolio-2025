Spinning Candle Display

An animation exercise focused on adding movement to an existing logo.


Process

Software: SolidWorks, Corel Draw Materials: 1/8" MDF, paper, spray paint, wood glue Timeline: 2 Weeks

1. Design in SolidWorks
Create individual pieces in Solidworks, ensuring thickness is extruded to 1/8" thick to mimic the MDF, and assemble pieces to create a digital prototype.


2. Transfer to Corel Draw
Import shapes into Corel Draw, and convert to vector format. Add winter scenes in the frame panels.


3. Assemble and Test
Assemble and glue all pieces together. At first, the candle was too heavy to spin, so I adjusted the fan material and size.
