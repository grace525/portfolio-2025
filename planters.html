Virtual Garden


Process

Software: Rhino
Timeline: 1 Week

1. Sketch and Ideate
Come up with the basic forms of various pots and plants, keeping in mind the various textures and opacities to display in the final design.


2. Create Forms and Add Textures
Reference sketches to build each individual element, adding textures and colors.


3. Assemble and Render
Arrange all elements into one composition and render the final image.
